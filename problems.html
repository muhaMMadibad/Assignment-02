Problems

  • Infrastructure and Urban Planning

    Inadequate infrastructure, including roads, public transportation, and sewage systems, has led to traffic congestion, flooding during monsoons, and other urban planning challenges.

    Water Shortage

    Karachi often experiences water shortages, and access to clean drinking water is a pressing issue for many residents.

    Education

    Despite some progress, access to quality education remains a challenge for many residents, especially in low-income areas.
    Political Instability
    Political instability and governance issues have sometimes hindered effective policymaking and urban management.
    Security And Crimes
    Karachi has experienced periods of high crime rates and political violence. While security has improved in recent years, crime remains a concern in certain areas.
